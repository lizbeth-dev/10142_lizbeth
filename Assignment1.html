Essential ingredients

ingredients

Protein: 1 kg of meat (beef or goat on the bone, cubed). Rice: 3 cups of aromatic, long-grain rice (preferably golden or regular Basmati). Aromatics & Broth: 3.5 cups of beef broth (water from boiling the meat), plus 1 tablespoon of minced garlic and 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger. Fats: 3 to 4 tablespoons of cooking oil or ghee. Vegetables: 2 to 3 large red onions, thinly sliced. Veggies (Optional): 3-4 medium potatoes, peeled and halved. Seasoning: Salt to taste

spices

spices used

Cumin (seeds or ground) Cardamom (pods or ground) Cinnamon (stick or ground) Cloves (whole or ground) Black Pepper (peppercorns or ground)
